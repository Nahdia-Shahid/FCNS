Editorial Roles & Responsibilities

1. Patron-in-Chief
Designation:
Vice Chancellor, GCWUF
Overview:
Provides institutional leadership and strategic oversight to ensure sustainability and compliance with national and international academic standards.
Responsibilities:
  • Provide overall academic and administrative patronage to the journal.
  • Endorse the journal’s vision, mission, and strategic direction.
  • Approve major editorial policies and senior appointments, where required.
  • Facilitate institutional support and ensure compliance with HEC regulations.
  • Promote the journal at national and international academic forums.
2. Editor-in-Chief
Designation:
Professor
Overview:
Responsible for maintaining the academic quality, integrity, and credibility of the journal.
Responsibilities:
  • Formulate and implement editorial policies in line with HEC, COPE, and international ethical standards.
  • Ensure a rigorous double-blind peer review process.
  • Make final decisions on manuscript acceptance or rejection.
  • Enforce plagiarism, AI usage, and publication ethics policies.
  • Ensure timely publication as per HEC requirements.
  • Lead editorial board meetings and evaluate editorial performance.
  • Represent the journal for indexing, recognition, and academic engagement.
  • Ensure compliance with HEC recognition and upgradation criteria.
3. Editors / Associate Editors
Designation:
Associate Professors
Overview:
Support the Editor-in-Chief in managing scholarly content and peer review.
Responsibilities:
  • Manage assigned manuscripts throughout the peer review process.
  • Identify and communicate with qualified reviewers.
  • Assess manuscripts for originality, relevance, and technical quality.
  • Recommend editorial decisions to the Editor-in-Chief.
  • Ensure timely completion of reviews and revisions.
  • Contribute to policy development, special issues, and quality assurance.
  • Promote ethical research and publication practices.
4. Managing Editor
Designation:
Associate Professor / Assistant Professor
Overview:
Oversees the operational and administrative management of the journal.
Responsibilities:
  • Manage day-to-day editorial and publication operations.
  • Coordinate communication among authors, reviewers, and editors.
  • Maintain manuscript records through the journal management system (e.g., OJS).
  • Ensure timely online publication and issue release.
  • Maintain plagiarism reports, reviewer databases, and editorial records.
  • Prepare reports and documentation required by HEC.
  • Ensure accuracy and regular updates of the journal website.
5. Editorial Board Members
Scope:
National & International
Overview:
Support academic quality and outreach of the journal.
Responsibilities:
  • Provide expert peer reviews in relevant subject areas.
  • Advise on editorial policy, scope, and quality enhancement.
  • Recommend qualified national and international reviewers.
  • Suggest themes, special issues, and emerging research areas.
  • Promote the journal within academic and professional networks.
  • Uphold confidentiality and ethical standards in the review process.
  • Participate in editorial meetings and consultations when required.
6. Advisory Board Members
Scope:
National & International
Overview:
Provides strategic guidance to strengthen the journal’s academic standing and visibility.
Responsibilities:
  • Advise on long-term academic strategy and quality improvement.
  • Provide guidance on international best practices and indexing requirements.
  • Support diversity and international representation on the editorial board.
  • Recommend improvements in editorial and peer review policies.
  • Enhance the journal’s national and international reputation.
