Archiving Policy – Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences

Policy Statement
The Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences (JCNS) is committed to the long-term preservation, accessibility, and availability of published scholarly content. The journal maintains an archiving policy to ensure that all published articles remain permanently accessible to researchers, educators, students, and the wider academic community.
Digital Preservation
All published articles are preserved in the journal's electronic archive to ensure continued access and long-term availability. JCNS is committed to maintaining secure digital records and protecting published content against accidental loss or technological changes.
Institutional Repository
Published articles may also be archived through the institutional repository of Government College Women University Faisalabad or other recognized academic repositories to support long-term preservation and wider dissemination of scholarly research.
Author Self-Archiving
Authors are encouraged to archive the published version of their articles in institutional repositories, personal websites, research networking platforms, or other non-commercial repositories, provided that appropriate citation to the original publication in JCNS is included.
Permanent Accessibility
Once published, articles remain permanently available through the journal's official website. The journal aims to provide uninterrupted access to all published issues, ensuring that scholarly content remains available for future generations of researchers.
Version Control
Any corrections, updates, or retractions made after publication will be clearly documented while preserving the integrity of the original scholarly record. Appropriate notices will be linked to the affected article to maintain transparency and accuracy.
Metadata Preservation
JCNS preserves essential bibliographic information, including article titles, author details, abstracts, keywords, publication dates, volume and issue numbers, and other metadata to support indexing, discoverability, and long-term retrieval of published research.
Future Preservation Services
As the journal continues to grow, JCNS intends to participate in recognized digital preservation initiatives and archiving services that support sustainable long-term access to scholarly publications and compliance with international publishing standards.
Access to Archived Content
Archived articles will remain freely accessible through the journal's website unless legal, ethical, or regulatory requirements necessitate otherwise. Any restrictions on access will be clearly communicated to readers.
Journal Commitment
JCNS is dedicated to preserving the scholarly record by ensuring the secure, reliable, and permanent availability of published research. The journal continuously reviews its archiving practices to align with international best practices in digital preservation and academic publishing.
