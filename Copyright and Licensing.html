Copyright & Licensing – Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences

Copyright Ownership
Authors retain the copyright of their published work. By submitting a manuscript, authors grant the Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences (JCNS) the non-exclusive right to publish, distribute, archive, and preserve the article in both electronic and printed formats.
Publishing License
Upon acceptance, authors grant JCNS permission to publish the final version of the manuscript on the journal website and through other authorized academic platforms. This license enables the journal to disseminate scholarly work while preserving the authors' intellectual property rights.
Author Rights
Authors may share the published version of their article through personal websites, institutional repositories, research networks, and academic profiles, provided that proper acknowledgment and citation of the original publication in JCNS are included.
Reader Permissions
Readers are permitted to access, download, read, print, and cite published articles for educational and research purposes. Any commercial use, redistribution, or modification of published material requires prior written permission from the journal and the copyright holder.
Third-Party Materials
Authors are responsible for obtaining written permission to reproduce any copyrighted figures, tables, illustrations, photographs, or other third-party materials included in their manuscripts. Appropriate acknowledgments must be provided within the article.
Licensing Policy
JCNS intends to adopt a recognized open-access licensing framework for future publications. The applicable license governing the use and distribution of each published article will be clearly stated on the article's publication page.
Reuse of Published Content
Proper citation of the original publication is mandatory whenever any portion of a published article is reused. Users must not misrepresent, alter, or misuse published content in ways that compromise the integrity of the scholarly record.
Author Responsibilities
Authors must ensure that submitted manuscripts are original and do not infringe upon the copyright or intellectual property rights of any individual, organization, or publisher. Any copyright disputes remain the responsibility of the submitting author(s).
Journal Responsibilities
JCNS is committed to protecting the intellectual property rights of authors while ensuring the widest possible dissemination of scholarly research. The journal follows internationally accepted publishing ethics and copyright practices.
Policy Updates
This Copyright & Licensing Policy may be revised periodically to reflect changes in publishing standards, legal requirements, and journal practices. Authors and readers are encouraged to review this policy regularly for the most current information.
