Complaints & Appeals – Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences

Policy Statement
The Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences (JCNS) is committed to maintaining fairness, transparency, and accountability throughout the editorial and publication process. Authors, reviewers, readers, and editors have the right to submit complaints or appeals regarding editorial decisions, publication ethics, peer review, or any aspect of the journal's operations. Every complaint and appeal is treated seriously, confidentially, and impartially.
Complaints
Complaints may relate to delays in the editorial process, peer review concerns, ethical misconduct, publication practices, conflicts of interest, plagiarism, copyright issues, or any matter affecting the quality and integrity of the journal. All complaints should provide sufficient information and supporting evidence to facilitate an objective investigation.
Appeals Against Editorial Decisions
Authors who believe that a manuscript has been rejected unfairly may submit a formal appeal to the Editor-in-Chief. Appeals must clearly explain the reasons for the request and provide scientific justification supporting reconsideration of the editorial decision. Appeals based solely on disagreement with reviewer comments without supporting evidence are unlikely to be accepted.
Review of Complaints and Appeals
Upon receiving a complaint or appeal, the Editorial Office will acknowledge receipt and conduct an independent review. Where necessary, additional reviewers, editorial board members, or external experts may be consulted to ensure an impartial and evidence-based evaluation.
Decision Process
Following a thorough investigation, the journal will communicate its decision to the concerned party. Depending on the findings, the journal may uphold the original decision, initiate further review, request revisions, issue corrections, or take other appropriate editorial actions consistent with international publication ethics.
Confidentiality
All complaints, appeals, supporting documents, and correspondence will be handled confidentially. Information will only be shared with individuals directly involved in the investigation to ensure fairness and protect the privacy of all parties.
Timely Resolution
JCNS aims to resolve complaints and appeals as promptly as possible. While the time required may vary depending on the complexity of the matter, every effort will be made to provide a fair and timely resolution.
Responsibilities of Complainants
Individuals submitting complaints or appeals are expected to communicate respectfully, provide accurate information, and refrain from making false, malicious, or defamatory allegations. Complaints submitted without sufficient evidence may not proceed to formal investigation.
Final Decision
The decision of the Editor-in-Chief, following completion of the journal's appeals procedure, shall be considered final. All decisions are made in accordance with the journal's editorial policies, publication ethics, and internationally recognized publishing standards.
Journal Commitment
JCNS is dedicated to providing a transparent, ethical, and accountable publication environment. The journal continuously strives to improve its editorial practices and welcomes constructive feedback that contributes to maintaining the highest standards of scholarly publishing.
