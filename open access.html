Open Access Policy – Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences

Open Access Statement
The Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences (JCNS) is committed to the principles of open access and believes that research should be freely available to researchers, educators, students, industry professionals, and the public worldwide. All articles published in JCNS are made immediately available online without subscription or access charges.
Free and Immediate Access
JCNS provides immediate, unrestricted, and permanent access to all published articles. Readers may read, download, copy, print, search, or link to the full text of articles without requesting prior permission from the publisher or the authors, provided that proper citation is given to the original work.
Benefits of Open Access
Open access publishing enhances the visibility, accessibility, and impact of scholarly research. By removing subscription barriers, JCNS supports the global exchange of scientific knowledge and encourages collaboration among researchers from academia, industry, and government organizations.
Author Rights
Authors retain the copyright of their published work while granting JCNS the right to publish, archive, and disseminate the article. Authors are encouraged to share the published version through institutional repositories, personal websites, and academic networking platforms with proper citation of the original publication.
Reader Permissions
Readers are permitted to access, download, distribute, and cite published articles for educational, research, and non-commercial purposes. Any commercial use or modification of published content requires prior written permission from the journal and the respective author(s).
Article Availability
All accepted manuscripts are published electronically on the official JCNS website and remain permanently accessible in the journal archive to ensure long-term preservation and availability of scholarly content.
Publication Quality
Open access does not influence editorial decisions. Every submission undergoes a rigorous double-blind peer review process based solely on originality, technical quality, significance, and relevance to the journal's scope.
Licensing Policy
JCNS intends to adopt an internationally recognized open-access licensing model for future publications. The applicable license for each article will be clearly displayed on the published version of the manuscript.
Archiving Policy
To ensure the long-term preservation of scholarly work, published articles will be archived through institutional repositories and journal archives. Additional preservation services may be adopted as the journal expands its indexing and digital preservation infrastructure.
Our Commitment
JCNS is dedicated to promoting the unrestricted dissemination of high-quality scientific research while maintaining the highest standards of publication ethics, transparency, integrity, and academic excellence.
