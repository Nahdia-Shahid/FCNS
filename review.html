Join as Reviewer – Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences

Become a Reviewer
The Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences (JCNS) invites qualified researchers, academicians, and industry experts to join our panel of reviewers. As a peer-reviewed, refereed journal, JCNS relies on the expertise and commitment of reviewers to ensure the quality, integrity, and relevance of published research.

Serving as a reviewer provides an opportunity to contribute to the advancement of knowledge in computing and numerical sciences while staying updated with the latest research developments.

Roles and Responsibilities

Reviewers are expected to
Evaluate manuscripts based on originality, technical quality, clarity, and relevance
Provide constructive and unbiased feedback to authors
Maintain confidentiality of submitted manuscripts
Identify ethical concerns such as plagiarism or duplicate submissions
Submit reviews within the specified time frame
Eligibility Criteria
Applicants should meet the following criteria
Hold a PhD or equivalent qualification in a relevant field (or be near completion)
Have a strong research background in computing, numerical sciences, or related disciplines
Possess at least 2–3 publications in reputable journals or conferences
Demonstrate expertise in one or more specialized areas
Areas of Expertise
We are currently seeking reviewers in, but not limited to
Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
Data Science and Big Data Analytics
Numerical Analysis and Scientific Computing
Software Engineering
Cybersecurity
Computational Modeling and Simulation
Emerging Technologies (HPC, Blockchain, IoT, etc.)
Benefits of Becoming a Reviewer
Recognition as an official reviewer of JCNS
Certificate of reviewing (upon request or annually)
Opportunity to enhance academic and professional profile
Early access to cutting-edge research
Networking opportunities with global researchers
Application Process
Interested candidates are requested to submit the following
Updated Curriculum Vitae (CV)
List of recent publications
Areas of expertise (keywords)
Institutional affiliation and contact details
Applications should be sent via
Email: fcns.journal@gcwuf.edu.pk
Subject Line: Application for Reviewer – JCNS
Review Process
All manuscripts are reviewed under a double-blind peer review system
Reviewers are assigned papers based on their expertise
A standard review form and guidelines will be provided
Code of Conduct
Reviewers must adhere to high ethical standards, including
Objectivity and fairness
Confidentiality
Avoidance of conflicts of interest
