Aim and Scope – Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences

Aim
The Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences (JCNS) aims to provide a high-quality international platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to publish original and impactful research in the fields of computing, numerical analysis, and their interdisciplinary applications. The journal is committed to advancing knowledge by promoting innovative methodologies, theoretical developments, and practical implementations that address real-world computational challenges.

JCNS seeks to foster collaboration between disciplines and bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical applications in science, engineering, and technology.

Interdisciplinary Nature

The journal particularly encourages submissions that integrate computing and numerical methods with other disciplines, promoting cross-disciplinary innovation and real-world problem solving.
