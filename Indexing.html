Indexing – Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences

Overview
The Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences (JCNS) is dedicated to enhancing the visibility, accessibility, and acdemic impact of published research. the journal actively pursues inclusion in reputable indexing and abstracting databases to ensure global dissemination of high-quality scholarly work.
Indexing Status
JCNS is currently in the process of applying for inclusion in recognized national and internations indexing and abstracting services. The journal aims to be indexed in the following databases:
Google Scholar
Directory of Open Access Journals (DOAJ)
CrossRef (for DOI registration)
Scopus (Elsevier)
Web of Science (Claricate Analytics)
EBSCOhost Databases
ProQuest databases
Index Copernicus International (ICI)
OpenAIRE
BASE (Bielefeld Academic Search Engine)
Digital Identification and Archiving
To ensure persistent accessibility and reliable citation of published content:

All accepted articles are assigned Digital Object Identifiers (DOIs) trhough CrossRef.
The journal follows established digitabl preservation and archiving practices to ensure long-term access to its content.
Open Access Visibility
JCNS follows an open-access oublishing model, ensuring that all published articles are freely accessible online. This approach significantly enhances citation potential, readership, and global research visibility through indexing platforms.
Quality Standards for Indexing
To meet the criteris of leading indexing services, JCNS striclty adheres to internationally recognized publishing standards, including:

A rigorous and transparent peer-review process
An experienced and academically qualified editorial board
Clear publication ethics and editorial policies
Consistent and timely publication scheduled
High-quality, original scholarly contributions
Continuous Improvement
JCNS is committed to continuous development and alignment with the evolving standards of major indexing databases. Updates regarding indexing status will be regularly published.
