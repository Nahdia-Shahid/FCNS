Patron-in-Chief's Message

The launch of the Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences marks a significant milestone for Government College Women University Faisalabad. JCNS is a dedicated platform for rigorous research work in computing, numerical analysis, artificial intelligence, and data science. The university is firmly committed to advancing these disciplines through credible, high-standard peer-reviewed publication.

A journal's credibility rests on consistent standards, transparent processes, and independent peer review. We invite researchers worldwide to contribute original work and engage with this growing scholarly platform. Our institutional conviction to JCNS is unwavering, and we look forward to its recognition as a trusted platform for scientific contribution.



PROF. DR. SAIMA AKRAM

Coordinator Faculty of Computing and Numerical Sciences

Editor-in-Chief's Message

I warmly welcome researchers and readers to the Journal of Computing and Numerical Sciences. JCNS is dedicated to advancing research work in computational theory, numerical methods, artificial intelligence, and data science. It is built on transparent editorial governance, rigorous double-blind peer review, and a firm commitment to originality and scientific merit.

As Editor-in-Chief, my goal is to serve both established and early-career scholars with fairness and intellectual rigor. The editorial board brings together expertise across core and emerging areas of computing and numerical sciences. I invite the global research community to contribute to shaping JCNS into a journal of lasting scholarly distinction.
