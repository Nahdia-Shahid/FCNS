Faculty of Computing and Numerical Science (FCNS) is an internationally peer-reviewed and indexed, Open Access journal that aims to disseminate scientific information to the international community through the publication of research articles, review papers, research notes, and communications in all subfields of Science and Technology.

Our Focus

Original Research Articles and Review Papers provide detailed and evaluative descriptions of the current status of knowledge and new trends in science and technology.
